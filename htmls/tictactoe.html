🐧 vs 🪨

The Penguins 🐧 and the Rocks 🪨 are at war!

Rules:

This is a game for TWO players. Each player chooses a team, the Penguins 🐧 or the Rocks 🪨.
Penguins 🐧 have feet, so the Penguin 🐧 player moves first.
Click on a spot to place your Penguin 🐧. The turn will pass to the patient Rock 🪨 player.

Each player will continue this maddening cycle of generational hatred until one side vanquishes the other by placing three of their soldiers in a straight line before the other, or when the inevitable attrition of the war leaves both sides in a stalemate with no moves let to make.

Rated M for Mature: violent themes and imagery
